Relics drop locations

How to get Lith M2 relics?

So, if you want to farm Lith M2 you want to choose between:

  • Void missions: Hepit[Capture], Stribog[Sabotage], Tarnis[Defense], Teshub[Exterminate], and Tiwaz[Mobile Defense].
  • Tier 2 Fortuna Bounty(Stage 2 or Stage 3)
  • Tier 2 Cetus Bounty(Stage 2 or Stage 3)

Where to farm Meso B3 relics?

If you want to farm Meso B3 you want to choose between:

  • Void missions: Ukko[Capture], Oxomoco [Exterminate], Stribog [Sabotage], Tiwaz [Mobile Defense], Belenus [Defense], Taranis [Defense], and Ani [Survival].
  • Tier 3 Fortuna Bounty(Stage 2 & 3 or Stage 4)
  • Tier 3 Cetus Bounty(Stage 2 & 3 or Stage 4)

How to get Neo N9 relics?

If you want to farm Neo N9 you want to choose between:

  • Void missions: Ukko[Capture], Oxomoco [Exterminate], Marduk [Sabotage], Aten [Mobile Defense], Belenus [Defense], Mithra [Interception], Mot [Survival], and Ani [Survival].
  • Tier 4 Fortuna Bounty(Stage 2 & 3 or Stage 4 or Stage 5)
  • Tier 4 Cetus Bounty(Stage 2 & 3 or Stage 4 or Stage 5)

Where to farm Axi S4 and Axi A5 relics?

And finally, if you want to farm Axi S4 or Axi A5 you want to choose between:

  • Void missions: Marduk [Sabotage], Aten [Mobile Defense], Belenus(Rotation C) [Defense], Mithra(Rotation C) [Interception], and Mot(Rotation C) [Survival].
  • Tier 5 Fortuna Bounty(Stage 2 & 3 or Stage 4 or Stage 5)
  • Tier 5 Cetus Bounty(Stage 2 & 3 or Stage 4 or Stage 5)
