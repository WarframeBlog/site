How To Get Boltor Prime

  • Warframe Blog
  • Primes
  • How To Get Boltor Prime
Published by warframe on July 26, 2019

Hey guys. And the Boltor Prime along with Ankyros Prime and Rhino Prime have emerged from the Prime Vault. Today I'll be showing you which relics you'll need to farm to get Boltor Prime and where you can farm these relics.

Boltor Prime Relics

So, Boltor Prime parts scattered across four different relics:

  • Lith B4 that drops Boltor Prime Blueprint
  • Axi S3 that drops Boltor Prime Stock
  • Neo R1 that drops Boltor Prime Receiver
  • Meso N6 that drops Boltor Prime Barrel

Boltor Prime Relics Farming

Before we proceed any further, I would like to say that all spots that I’ll share with you are based on my personal experience farming Boltor Prime relics. The spots gave me the necessary relics at the shortest time, but you may have a different result as relic drops are also based on a chance. Now, let’s get on to the how to farm Boltor Prime relics fast and easy.

How To Get Boltor Prime Receiver Relics

For Neo R1 Relics, my recommendation is Belenus in the Void. Belenus is a straightforward defense mission best attempted with a team and you should be able to get two Neo relics in ten waves most of the time(or one Meso and one Neo).

How To Get Boltor Prime Blueprint Relics

For farming Lith B4 Relics the Hepit mission in the Void is a great option. It’s a quick capture mission which you can repeat pretty quickly.

How To Get Boltor Prime Stock Relics

You can obtain Axi S3 Relics from Marduk - a sabotage mission. This mission has a chance to drop Neo relics as well.

How To Get Boltor Prime Barrel Relics

In order to farm Meso N6 Relics, I would recommend Ukko in the Void. It’s a capture mission and can be completed really quickly.

Finally, relic packs always a decent way to get your hands on those new Boltor Prime relics.

And that’s pretty much all I want to say about Boltor Prime Relics Farming. I hope this guide helped and good luck with farming. Bye-bye.
